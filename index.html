What is Free Fire Advance Server?

The Free Fire Advance Server is a beta testing platform where selected players can try out new features, characters, and game modes before they are officially released. This server allows players to provide feedback, helping developers improve the overall game experience for everyone.

Why Join Free Fire Advance Server?

  • New Characters: Test the abilities of upcoming characters.
  • Exclusive Rewards: Earn rewards such as in-game diamonds for providing feedback.
  • Influence Development: Help improve gameplay by sharing feedback directly with developers.

How to Register for Free Fire Advance Server

  1. Visit the official website: ff-advance.ff.garena.com
  2. Log in with your Facebook account linked to Free Fire.
  3. Fill out the registration form and wait for approval.
  4. Receive your unique Activation Code if selected.

Note: Activation codes are limited and only selected players will receive them.

Download Free Fire Advance Server APK
